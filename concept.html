Creative Industrial & Product Designer from China. Highly motivated and inspired by many small and interesting moments hidden in daily life. Strategic and dialectical thinker, highly creative hands on designer, results oriented leader and team player, with demonstrated ability to conceive and implement new ideas and strategies.

Guorong Luo has passion about telling people stories inside his design and has abundant experience working with designers from different countries, helping him adapt to the new environment or change easily. He also have a wide range of interests in many areas such as Graphic design, Web design, Open source, 3D printing, Stage design, Photography and Wood making.
